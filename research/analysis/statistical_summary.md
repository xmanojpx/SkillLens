# Statistical Analysis Summary

**Dataset**: 100 student responses

## Key Findings

1. Only 24.0% of students know why they were rejected
2. 76.0% feel current guidance is generic and not personalized
3. Only 26.0% are aware of ATS systems
4. Students with ATS awareness have 5.9x higher shortlisting rates
5. 100.0% believe an AI-based tool would help them
6. Average success rate is only 9.53% (shortlists/applications)

| Applications | 40.5 | 40.5 | 14.50 |
| Shortlists | 2.34 | 1.5 | 2.47 |
